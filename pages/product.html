What We Do

In the realm of product management, our club is equally committed to nurturing talent. We offer product management services to various organizations, including startups and corporate firms. To cultivate the skills of aspiring product managers, we conduct specialized workshops and invite seasoned professionals to share their experiences through fireside chats. These initiatives are designed to equip students with the expertise needed to excel in the dynamic field of product management.

ProdStorm

The Product Management division at BHCG is pleased to announce the second edition of Prodstorm, our flagship product deck challenge at ATMOS. Building on the first edition's success, this year's Prodstorm aims to expand its scale and impact.


Participants will extend existing product functionalities or develop new products. Their ingenuity, feasibility, and prioritization will be evaluated by seasoned product executives.


Prodstorm provides fresh insights to our partners and a platform for participants to enhance their product management skills. Prodstorm also offers networking opportunities, connecting participants with industry experts and like-minded peers

PREREQUISITES

Registration of all participants

SUMMARIES

Participants are required to summarise their ideas within two pages

DECKS

Shortlisted candidates will proceed to craft a detailed deck of their solution

FINALS

Winning decks get an opportunity to present to the jury and a chance to win prizes.
